The New Holi

Hinduism demands women renounce earthly pleasures after the death of their husbands. Thus celebrating Holi has been forbidden for Hindu widows for 400 years. But for the last couple of years in Vrindavan - NGO Sulabh international organized a special gathering for widowed women to celebrate Holi. Read more to hear about their experiences and thoughts.
